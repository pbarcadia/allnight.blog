▶️ ⚗️ Alchemy (Click to Expand)

Alchemy provides massive stat multipliers. It offers critical survival tools. Max your skill to 225. This supports your dungeon groups.

⭐ Essential Alchemist Items & Perks:

  • 💎 Philosopher's Stone: This is a powerful trinket option. It requires character level 20. It requires Alchemy skill 200. It grants +5 to all spell resistances. This is an incredible stat stick for Phase 2.
  • 🧠 Mixology (Passive): This is a permanent passive ability. It enhances your active consumables. It increases the stat bonuses of your elixirs. It also doubles their duration. This perk is unique to Alchemists.

⭐ Top Phase 2 Craftables:

  • 🧪 Elixir of Lesser Agility: Gives +15 Agility for 1 hour. This is the top offensive elixir for Rogues. It is also best for Hunters.
  • 🧪 Shadow Protection Potion: Absorbs 1125 shadow damage. This is a mandatory safety item. Use it inside Scarlet Monastery: Graveyard.
  • 🧪 Elixir of Firepower: Increases fire spell damage by 10. This spikes Fire Mage burst. It also helps Destruction Warlocks.
  • 🔥 Elemental Fire: This is a vital crafting recipe. It unlocks at level 25. Sell this on the shared Auction House. It yields massive gold returns.

Blacksmithing allows you to forge powerful mail armor and physical weapons. Maxing your skill to 225 supplies core combat gear for Paladins, Warriors, Shamans, and Hunters. It also creates essential temporary weapon enhancements.

⭐ Essential Blacksmith Items & Utility:

  • 🪨 Heavy Sharpening Stone / 🪨 Heavy Weightstone: Unlocks at skill level 25. These items grant a flat temporary damage bonus to sharp or blunt melee weapons. They are mandatory consumables for all physical DPS classes.
  • 🔑 Silver Skeleton Key: Allows Blacksmiths to bypass locked doors and chests up to level 20. It provides excellent lockpicking utility inside early dungeons without needing a Rogue.

⭐ Top Phase 2 Craftable Gear:

  • 🛡️ Shining Silver Breastplate: Requires character level 29. This rare mail chest piece provides massive armor and raw stat combinations. It is the absolute best-in-slot crafted chest for Paladins and Shamans.
  • 🔨 Iridescent Hammer: Requires character level 28. A rare one-handed mace option. It serves as a top-tier physical or hybrid weapon weapon choice for the bracket ceiling.
  • 🥾 Green Iron Boots: Unlocks at level 29. Excellent baseline uncommon mail boots that fill an otherwise empty equipment slot for mail wearers.
