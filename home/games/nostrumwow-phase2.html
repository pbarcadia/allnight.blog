Phase 2 Progression Guide: Levels 20-29

Welcome to the definitive progression guide for Phase 2 of NostrumWoW. Shifting from the level 19 bracket to level 29 alters the mechanical landscape of the server. This article outlines the optimal dungeon progression path, critical pre-Raid/pre-BiS milestones, and target encounters up to Scarlet Monastery: Graveyard using 3.3.5 data parameters.

1. Phase 2 Core Milestones

Before stepping into a dungeon, every player should secure these baseline open-world upgrades the moment they cross the level 20 threshold:

  • Apprentice Riding (Level 20): Unlocks 60% ground mounts. Costs 4 gold base before reputation discounts. Essential for reducing dungeon run-back times and shifting the pace of world PvP.
  • Class Capability Spikes: Level 20 and level 24 introduce primary ability ranks and mechanics (such as a Rogue's poisons, a Druid's Cat Form scaling, or a Paladin's initial defensive auras).

2. Dungeon Progression Route & Target Loot

3. Profession Thresholds & Perks (Skill Cap 225)

In Phase 2, your max profession skill rises to 225. This is the Expert rank. Gathering skills give powerful passive bonuses in patch 3.3.5. Crafting provides great gear upgrades.

A. Gathering Profession Bonuses

Profession Passive Skill Name Bonus at 225 Skill Combat Value

Skinning Master of Anatomy (Rank 3) +9 Critical Strike Rating Great for Rogues, Hunters, and Feral Druids.
Herbalism Lifeblood (Rank 4) Heals 72-216 HP & grants +50 Haste An extra instant heal. The Haste lasts for 20 seconds.

B. High-Value Crafting Milestones

  • Engineering (225): You can craft Green Tinted Goggles. They give +8 Stamina and +8 Intellect or Spirit. You also unlock Iron Grenades. These provide a crucial 3-second AoE stun for dungeon pulls and PvP.
  • First Aid (225): You can learn to craft Mageweave Bandages. They heal for 1,100 health over 8 seconds. This is huge self-healing for every class.
  • Tailoring (225): Allows crafting of Silk armor sets and heavy bags for your bank slots.

4. Class Power Spikes & Talents at Level 29

Reaching level 29 gives you exactly 20 talent points. You can reach the 15-point talent tier. You can also mix points into secondary trees for utility. Here is what to highlight for each role.

A. Melee & Physical DPS (Rogues, Warriors, Hunters)

  • Rogues: You unlock Poisons via the level 20 class quest. At level 29, Combat Rogues can grab Blade Flurry for excellent AoE damage in dungeons. Subtlety Rogues unlock Preparation to reset cooldowns.
  • Warriors: Arms Warriors can spec into Sweeping Strikes. This heavily boosts your multi-target cleave damage. You also unlock Intercept in Berserker Stance at level 30, making level 29 the peak of your Arms/Fury utility balance.
  • Hunters: Beast Mastery Hunters can grab Intimidation for an on-demand pet stun. Marksmanship unlocks Aimed Shot to stop enemy healing in PvP.

B. Casters & Ranged Magic (Mages, Warlocks, Priests)

  • Mages: Fire Mages can pick up Blast Wave for solid AoE damage and knockbacks. Frost Mages grab Cold Snap to reset defensive frost cooldowns instantly.
  • Warlocks: Affliction builds can secure Siphon Life for passive healing. Destruction specs gain Pyroclasm or push deep for high Searing Pain crit multipliers.
  • Priests: Shadow Priests get Mind Flay ranks that deal high single-target shadow damage. You can also grab Silence in the Shadow tree for a 5-second lock on enemy casters.

C. Hybrids & Healers (Paladins, Druids, Shamans)

  • Paladins: Protection Paladins can unlock Holy Shield to hit the uncrushable avoidance cap against dungeon bosses. Retribution gains Repentance for long-range crowd control.
  • Druids: Resto specs unlock Swiftmend for instant emergency group healing. Feral Druids get massive power boosts from Predatory Strikes in Cat Form.
  • Shamans: Restoration Shamans unlock Mana Tide Totem. This solves your group's mana issues in longer dungeon fights. Elemental Shamans can spec into Elemental Mastery for guaranteed critical bursts.

5. Open World PvE, Server Features & Economy

NostrumWoW uses a dedicated cross-faction framework. World PvP is uncommon. Focus your open-world strategy on group mechanics, custom server rewards, and the neutral economy.

A. Cross-Faction Cooperation & Grouping

  • Dungeon Finder (RDF): The queue system automatically matches Horde and Alliance players together. This ensures near-instant queue times for dungeons.
  • Manual Grouping: You can form mixed-faction groups manually in the open world. Use the command /invite CharacterName to invite the opposite faction.
  • Global Chat Channel: Both factions share a single, global world chat. Use this shared space to coordinate dungeon runs up to SM Graveyard.

B. Server Milestones & Auction Economy

  • Level 20 Gold Mail: The server automatically mails you a gold reward when you reach level 20. This fully covers your Apprentice Riding and mount costs.
  • Shared Auction House: The Auction House is completely unified across factions. Alliance listings can be bought by Horde players instantly.
  • Trading Restriction: Direct face-to-face cross-faction trading is disabled due to client limits. Use the shared Auction House to swap items.

6. Elite Quest Rewards & Faction Reputation

Guaranteed blue-quality items come from completing long-form zone storylines. Do not skip these primary quest chains.

A. Iconic Faction Rings

  • Seal of Wrynn (Alliance): Acquired via the extensive Missing Diplomat quest chain. Provides +4 to all primary attributes.
  • Seal of Sylvanas (Horde): Acquired from the Arugal Must Die quest chain inside SFK. Provides +8 Stamina and +3 Intellect.

B. Warsong Gulch Rep Items (Level 28)

Battlegrounds feature mixed-faction teams to accelerate queue times. Reach Honored with your faction's Warsong vendor to unlock premium level 28 slots:

  • Lorekeeper's Ring / Advisor's Ring: Best-in-slot rings for spell power users.
  • Legionnaire's Band / Sentinel's Medallion: Essential rings for raw agility and stamina.
  • Warsong Outrider's Cloak / Caretaker's Cape: Premier physical and caster capes for the level 29 bracket.

9. Phase 2 Consumables Checklist

Keep these consumables stocked in your bags. They prevent wipes during deep pulls in Scarlet Monastery: Graveyard.

A. Potions & Reagents

  • Greater Healing Potion: Restores 455 to 585 health instantly. Requires Alchemy skill 155.
  • Greater Mana Potion: Restores 455 to 585 mana instantly. Essential for healers in SM Graveyard.
  • Thistle Tea (Rogues Only): Restores 100 Energy instantly. Requires Cooking skill 60. Essential burst utility.

B. Stat Food & Elixirs

  • Heavy Crocolisk Stew: Gives +8 Stamina and +8 Spirit for 15 minutes. Best well-fed buff for physical classes.
  • Sagefish Delight: Restores 6 mana per 5 seconds for 15 minutes. Premium choice for Mages and Priests.
  • Elixir of Agility: Increases Agility by 15 for 1 hour. Premium physical damage elixir.
  • Elixir of Greater Defense: Increases armor by 150 for 1 hour. Essential for tanks pulling large packs.

10. Preparing for Phase 3

Phase 2 lasts for a limited time. Use the final days of the level 29 cap to prepare your character for the level 39 expansion.

  • Stockpile Materials: Gather Mageweave Cloth, Iron Ore, and Heavy Leather. You will need these to instantly break through the 225 profession cap when Phase 3 launches.
  • Bank Your Gold: Mount costs scale up in the next phase. Keep farming dungeons and selling profession cooldowns to build a stable gold reserve.
  • Max Your Reputations: Ensure you hit Honored or Revered with core factions to secure immediate access to upcoming tier gear.
