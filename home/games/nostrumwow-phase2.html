The journey expands. New milestones await your hero.

Welcome to Phase 2 of NostrumWoW. This phase covers levels 20 to 29. The world opens up dramatically. Major mechanical updates unlock immediately at level 20. Prepare your character for new battlegrounds, faster travel, and advanced crafting thresholds.

🚀 Big Server Updates: Level 20 Milestones

Reaching level 20 alters your gameplay experience. Three massive features unlock instantly.

🏇

60% Ground Mounts Unlocked

Say goodbye to walking. You can now learn Apprentice Riding. This boosts your open-world travel speed significantly. It drastically reduces dungeon run-back times.
🚩

Arathi Basin Battleground

The fight for resources begins. Arathi Basin officially opens its gates at level 20. Queue up safely using our cross-faction system. Earn honor and target powerful battleground gear rewards.
🛠️

Expert Professions Available

Your crafting limits expand. You can now visit specialized trainers to unlock Expert level professions. This raises your maximum skill cap to 225. It unlocks superior gear recipes and game-changing combat passives.
