⚔️ Nostrum: Phase 2 ⚔️

Welcome to Phase 2 of NostrumWoW. This phase covers levels 20 to 29. The world opens up dramatically. Major mechanical updates unlock immediately at level 20. Prepare your character for new battlegrounds. Get ready for faster travel and advanced crafting thresholds.

🚀 Big Server Updates: Level 20 Milestones

Reaching level 20 alters your gameplay experience. Three massive features unlock instantly.

Icon Milestone Description
🏇 60% Ground Mounts Say goodbye to walking. Learn Apprentice Riding. This boosts travel speed significantly. It reduces dungeon run-back times.

🛠️ Expert Professions Your crafting limits expand. Visit trainers to unlock expert skill caps. This raises your maximum skill cap to 225. It unlocks superior recipes.
