¶ 🧩 Nostrum WoW Recommended Addons 🛠️

Enhance your game experience on Nostrum WoW with this curated collection of active addons and user interface setups. These addons are verified to work with the WotLK 3.3.5a client.

¶ 🎒 Personal Addon List

Here is my active addon list. I use these for raiding. They help with leveling. Here are the links and details:

Addon Name & Link Description
📖 Ackis Recipe List Scans professions. Lists missing recipes. Shows where to find them.
🎒 AdiBags Filters and partitions your inventory items into distinct, smart categories within one clean window block.
💎 AtlasLoot Enhanced Browse loot tables. Check dungeons and raids in-game.
🪙 Auctionator Fixes the Auction House. Makes posting items easy. Scans market prices fast.
🍺 Bartender 4 Replaces action bars. Gives layout control. Customizes keybindings.
🗺️ Carbonite Quest and map overhaul. Adds an alternate map. Shows fast leveling routes.
📍 Carbonite (CustomWaypoints Edition) Integrates enhanced custom waypoint tracking features directly into the core Carbonite map engine architecture.
🖱️ Clique Enables seamless click-casting configuration directly onto your unit frames or 3D world targets.
⚔️ Compact Nameplates Replaces default nameplates. Uses clean bars. Improves combat visibility.
📊 Details! Damage Meter Analyzes combat logs. Tracks DPS in real-time. Shows damage and healing.
⏳ Elkano's BuffBars Replaces buff icons. Uses detailed progress bars.
⚙️ GearScore Lite Calculates a numeric ranking score for equipment setups to evaluate performance itemization levels quickly.
🟩 Grid2 Grid-based unit frames. Great for healers. Shows health clearly.
⏱️ OmniCC Adds cooldown text. Numbers show on action bars.
🔍 pfQuest Lightweight quest helper. Includes an in-game database.
💬 Prat 3.0 Chat frame addon. Adds custom colors. Shows timestamps. Copies URLs easily.
❓ Questie Puts quests on your map. Tracks leveling progress.
🗑️ Scrap Sells grey junk automatically. Repairs gear at vendors.
🐉 SilverDragon Scans the local environment context to alert you with precise popups upon locating rare creatures or spawns.
👁️ TellMeWhen Provides highly customizable visual notifications for tracking cooldowns, buffs, debuffs, and rotational procs.
⚓ Titan Panel Adds a top info bar. Tracks gold and bag space. Shows XP and durability.
✉️ WoW Instant Messenger (WIM) Isolates whispers. Uses clean pop-up windows.
👤 X-Perl UnitFrames Revamps unit frames. Adds 3D portraits. Shows clear health bars.
